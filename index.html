Breaking the Loop

A short walk through why your mind keeps replaying the same thing, and three real ways to interrupt it.

One

What Rumination Is

Rumination is a repetitive thinking pattern that keeps your mind returning to the same distressing event without ever reaching a resolution. It is not the same as reflection. Reflection moves you somewhere. Rumination keeps circling the same spot.

Two

Why It Keeps Replaying

Your brain treats an unresolved event like an open file it cannot close. Something about it, what it meant, what happens next, what it says about you, feels unfinished. Until that gets addressed directly, the loop keeps reopening on its own.

One Journaling Exercise

Write down the one thing on your mind right now. Just the facts, what happened, in a sentence or two. Getting it out of your head and onto the page is the point, not solving it.
